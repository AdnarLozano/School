Display the Cost of a Movie Ticket

Chapter 2 | Programming Challenge 3

This page will decide the cost of a movie ticket based on age.

The following list shows associated ticket cost based on the age of the customer:

  • Under age 5 (age < 5) cost is $0.00
  • Between ages 5 and 12 (5 >= age ≤ 12) cost is $5.00
  • Older than 12 (age > 12) cost is $9.00
