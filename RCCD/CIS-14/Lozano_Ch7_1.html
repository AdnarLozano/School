This page asks the user to enter two positive numbers. It will then calculate the values of: the first number raised to the power of the second number, the area of a right triangle with the first number as the base and the second number as the height, and the value of the hypoteneuse or the distance between any two points from an origin (0,0) and the coordinates of the two entered numbers (x,y), where the first number is the x-value and the second number is the y-value.
